Neurosciences & Neurosurgery

Neurosurgery at WeCare hospital is the speciality concerned with the surgical treatment of diseases of the brain, spinal cord and spinal column, trauma as well as the nerves. Among medical specialties, neurosurgery is, by far one of the most technically advanced.

The advent of techniques of minimally invasive surgery have contributed to a much shorter hospital stay and heightened patient comfort, while maximizing the efficiency of the procedure.

The Department of Neurosurgery at WeCare hospital is fully equipped to perform all types of surgeries for a wide range of illnesses.
